About Our Community

Mason Oaks is a charming residential community nestled in the heart of Wake Forest, North Carolina. Developed by NVR, Inc. (Ryan Homes) and established in the early 2020s, our neighborhood combines modern living with the warm character of North Carolina.

Our thoughtfully designed community features beautiful single-family homes with various floor plans to accommodate different lifestyles. The architecture reflects traditional Southern charm with contemporary amenities, creating an inviting atmosphere for all residents.

Mason Oaks is professionally managed by PPM Real Estate (Professional Property Management), ensuring that our community maintains high standards and operates smoothly for all homeowners.

Prime Location

Mason Oaks boasts an enviable location in Wake Forest, offering residents the perfect balance of suburban tranquility and urban convenience. Our community is just minutes away from:

  • Historic Downtown Wake Forest with charming shops and restaurants
  • Easy access to US-1 and I-540 for commuters
  • Proximity to Triangle Town Center and other shopping destinations
  • Quality schools in the Wake County Public School System
  • Beautiful parks and recreational areas
  • Just 30 minutes from downtown Raleigh
